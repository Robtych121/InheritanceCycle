What is The Inheritance Cycle?

The Inheritance Cycle (previously known as the Inheritance Trilogy) is a series of high-fantasy novels by homeschooled author Christopher Paolini. The series follows the exploits of Eragon, a teenage fosterling who becomes the first of a new order of Dragon Riders. The Author

Christopher Paolini was born on November 17, 1983 in Southern California. Christopher was homeschooled by his parents, often writing short stories and poems in attempt to put his thoughts into words. He made frequent trips to the library and read widely.
